ABOUT

The journey of achieving my goals has been a very challenging one. Nothing has been handed to me and I am grateful that life presented me this experience. I have realized, “expect the unexpected”, is more than just a saying and has been a motivator each and every day. When presented negativity, never should one give up and must continue to press toward the mark. At a young age, people have recognized my creative mind set and encouraged me to pursue my passion beginning from participating in architecture classes in high school.

It came to realization that this is what I have a passion for doing. Being able to think, create, and call a design my own has provided me with a sense of contentment. I am confident in what I do and that played a huge role into shaping me into the individual I am today. One more year is all I have left in college, but my goal still remains the same trying my best to obtain as much knowledge about my profession as I can. Confronted with new challenges every day, I am ready to attack them head on fulfilling my dream as an architect.
